1. Overview

At INDIA ELECTRONICS HUB SERVICE CENTRE, customer satisfaction is our top priority. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for our TV repair services in Lucknow. We are committed to ensuring fair and transparent dealings with all our customers.

2. Inspection & Diagnosis Fee

  • A nominal inspection/visiting charge may apply when our technician visits your location for diagnosis.
  • This fee covers the technician's travel and initial diagnosis, and is non-refundable once the technician has visited and inspected the TV.
  • If you cancel the appointment before the technician is dispatched, no inspection fee will be charged.

3. Cancellation Before Repair

You have full flexibility before any repair work begins:

  • After diagnosis, our technician will provide a detailed cost estimate for the repair.
  • You are free to decline the repair at this stage. Only the inspection fee (if applicable) will be charged.
  • No repair work will commence without your explicit approval of the quoted price.
  • If you cancel the service before diagnosis, no charges will apply (provided the technician has not yet been dispatched).

4. Refund After Repair Completion

Since TV repair is a service-based offering, refunds after completion are handled case-by-case:

✅ Eligible for Refund / Free Re-Repair

  • Same issue reoccurs within the warranty period — we will re-repair at no cost or provide a refund for the repair charges
  • Repair not completed as described — if the agreed repair was not performed correctly, we will fix it free of charge or refund the service fee
  • Overcharging — if you were charged more than the agreed estimate without prior communication, the excess amount will be refunded

❌ Not Eligible for Refund

  • New or different issues that are unrelated to the original repair
  • Physical damage caused after the repair (drops, impacts, liquid spills)
  • Power surge or voltage fluctuation damage after repair
  • Tampering by another technician or unauthorized service provider
  • Natural wear and tear or age-related degradation of components
  • Repair completed and approved by the customer where the TV is functioning as expected

5. Parts Replacement Refund

  • If a replacement part is found to be defective within the warranty period, we will replace it at no additional cost or refund the parts charge.
  • Parts warranty covers manufacturing defects only, not damage from misuse, power surges, or physical impact.
  • Original defective parts that were replaced become the property of INDIA ELECTRONICS HUB SERVICE CENTRE unless otherwise agreed.

To request a refund or raise a complaint, follow these steps:

1

2

Provide Service Details

3

4

Refund Processing

If approved, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days via UPI, bank transfer, or the original payment method.

If you are unsatisfied with the resolution provided, you may escalate the matter:

  • Request a senior review by calling our helpline at 8005462960.
  • We will assign a senior team member to review your case and provide a final resolution within 7 working days.
  • All disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India.
